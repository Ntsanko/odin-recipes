Bunny Chow

Mutton bunny chow

A bunny chow is basically made from half a loaf of bread, though one can go smaller as needed. The bread must be hollowed out to make space for mutton or vegetable curry, though beef or chicken will work in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 1kg lamb pieces
  • Oil
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 green Cardamoms
  • 4 Cloves
  • A sprig of curry leaves
  • 1 tsp crushed green chillies
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • half tsp Tumeric
  • 2 tbles crushed Ginger and Garlic
  • 1 tbles Vinegar
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 3 tsp medium Chilli powder
  • 2 tsp Cumin powder
  • 2 blended Tomatoes
  • 5 small quartered potatoes
  • Chopped fresh Coriander
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cover pot's base with oil
  2. Heat oil
  3. Add Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Bay leaf and Fennel
  4. Stir
  5. Add curry leaves, chillies, onion, tumeric, ginger and Garlic
  6. Braise for 3-4 minutes
  7. Add spices, vinegar and sugar then mix well
  8. Add lamb pieces and salt
  9. Mix until meat is coated well
  10. Cover pot and cook/braise in moderate heat for a few minutes
  11. Stir until meat is well braised. Add potatoes
  12. Add water as needed to make the curry saucy
  13. When potatoes are almost cooked, add tomatoes
  14. When potatoes are almost soft, stir and add Coriander
  15. Fill hollow part of bread and serve with salad
